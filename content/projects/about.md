## About

I am a student researcher working across interaction design, information systems, architectural representation, and digital learning tools.

My current work focuses on how people search, compare, remember, and make decisions with digital interfaces. I am especially interested in project archives, teaching work samples, educational systems, and small tools that make complex information easier to inspect.

## Interests

- Information design and interface archives
- Architectural pedagogy and representation
- Digital learning systems
- Lightweight web tools for research and teaching
