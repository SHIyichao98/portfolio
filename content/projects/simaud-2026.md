## Overview

Shape grammars can generate many floor-plan variants, but designers still need ways to understand which rules and rule combinations affect building performance. This project links a small apartment floor-plan grammar with daylight simulation and explainable machine learning.

Each generated plan is encoded as a rule-sequence feature vector and evaluated through daylight performance metrics. A surrogate model supports rapid screening, while explainable AI methods identify rule features associated with stronger predicted performance.

## Research Focus

- Shape grammar for floor-plan generation
- Daylight simulation and performance-aware screening
- XGBoost surrogate modeling
- Rule-level reasoning and explainable AI
