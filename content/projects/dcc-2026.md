## Overview

This research investigates shape grammar inference from vector CAD drawings. Rather than assuming already-labeled symbolic inputs, the project treats CAD geometry as a difficult source that must be stabilized into recurring units, relation classes, and executable local rule schemata.

The work uses benchmark-referenced cases to test an unsupervised clustering pipeline. It connects manual analysis traditions in shape grammar research with computational procedures for decomposition, labeling, and local rule recovery.

## Research Focus

- Shape grammar inference from CAD geometry
- Unsupervised clustering for local relation classes
- Graph and spatial-relation representations
- Prototype workflows for rule-schema recovery
