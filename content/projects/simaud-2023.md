## Overview

This research studies how conditional shape embedding can be integrated with generative adversarial networks to evaluate raster-format architectural sketches. The project connects geometric representation, learned visual features, and design assessment.

## Research Focus

- Conditional shape embedding
- Generative adversarial networks
- Raster-format architectural sketches
- Computational evaluation of design representations
