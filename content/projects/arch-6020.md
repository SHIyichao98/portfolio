## Overview

This page collects teaching work samples from ARCH 6020. The work emphasizes computational design methods, iterative model making, and the translation of abstract rules into spatial systems.

The images are currently organized as a teaching archive rather than a polished case study. You can keep expanding this Markdown file with course context, assignment prompts, student learning goals, and your specific role in the class.

## Teaching focus

- Parametric and rule-based design thinking
- Morphological variation and aggregation
- Diagramming spatial logics
- Moving between model, drawing, rendering, and critique
