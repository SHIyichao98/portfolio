## Overview

This project explores how game-based interaction can support the evaluation and communication of architectural experience. The work uses mobile interaction, spatial tasks, and visual feedback to connect user experience with architectural qualities.

## Research Focus

- Architectural experience evaluation
- Serious games and mobile interaction
- User-centered spatial feedback
- Interface design for architectural research
