## Overview

This project investigates how emotional response, environmental design, and virtual reality can inform experience-oriented design research. The work connects neuro-design methods with immersive simulation as a way to evaluate and refine spatial experience.

## Research Focus

- Neuro design and environmental experience
- Virtual reality for design evaluation
- Emotion-centered tourist experience design
- Interface and visualization methods for spatial research
