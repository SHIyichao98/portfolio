## Overview

Dougong bracket sets are structurally and culturally significant elements in traditional Chinese architecture. Their geometry is rich, rule-bound, and difficult to describe through ordinary parametric modeling alone. This project revisits Dougong as a computational design problem.

The research develops a parametric specification of bracket-set design in Shape Machine. It focuses on how a design language can preserve dimensional control while supporting systematic variation, replacement, and recombination of components.

## Research Focus

- Dougong bracket-set typology
- Parametric shape grammar specification
- Shape Machine workflows for architectural components
- Rule-based control of traditional construction logic
