## Overview

Traditional Chinese gardens combine spatial sequence, cultural symbolism, landscape composition, and architectural detail. This project investigates how an interactive generative workflow can support garden design without flattening those relationships into generic image prompts.

The research proposes a user-centered process for generating and refining masterplan ideas. It brings together design analysis, structured inputs, interface thinking, and Stable Diffusion to create a more controllable design process for landscape architecture.

## Research Focus

- Chinese garden composition and spatial logic
- Interactive control for AI-assisted masterplan generation
- Design analysis as a bridge between precedent and generative output
- Human-in-the-loop workflows for landscape architecture
